About Me

Hello! Thank you for taking the time to view my very first website which I created from scratch. I am an aspiring full-stack developer trying to attain my certificate of completion from the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp. I was born in Taiwan and grew up in Toronto and Michigan. I attained my B.A in Political Science and Economics from the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor and received my M.B.A from the University of Texas at Austin - McCombs School of Business. I have been a Web/Mobile Applications Product Manager for the last 10 years.

In my personal time I enjoy travelling and being out in nature. I am an avid backpacker. My most recent adventure was taking 2 weeks to hike the Walker Haute Route from Mont Blanc France to Zermatt Switzerland. Other notable backpacking/camping trips was taking 6 weeks to hike/camp the Rocky Mountains from Grand Teton National Park to Jasper National Park in Canada.
